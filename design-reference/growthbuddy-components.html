Spacing

4px base unit. Section padding clamp(72px, 10vw, 120px) vertical · 24px gutter (20px ≤768).

Foundations

Radii

Pills are fully round; cards 20px, feature panels 24px, the hero CTA card 28px.

Foundations

Shadows / elevation

Neutral soft shadows for resting cards; warm rgba(74,47,23,…) ink for lifted chrome (nav, mega, pill hover). Never combine a hairline border with a drop shadow.

Component

FAQ accordion

Native <details>, hairline rows, a clay plus icon that rotates 135° to ×. Ships with FAQPage JSON-LD. One row open by default.

Where do the team members work?
They work remotely, aligned to your business hours, so they're online exactly when your team is — they join your meetings, use your tools, and live in your Slack channels day to day.
Who employs them?
We do — every team member is officially employed by Growthbuddy and sits on our payroll, so the contracts, taxes and compliance never land on your desk.
What if it doesn't work out?
We step in and find you a replacement at no extra cost — and because you only pay while someone is active, you're never locked in. See pricing →
